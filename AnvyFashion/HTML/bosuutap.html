Bộ sưu tập thu đông 2012

Tonight’s amfAR’s Cinema Against AIDS gala in Cannes is considered the hottest party ticket during the film festival, so needless to say, all fashion stops are pulled. The transparency trend has been creeping onto the red carpet recently—on Beyonce and Marc Jacobs at the Met Ball, and Kristen Stewart during her Snow White and the Huntsman world tour—and it made another appearance at the gala tonight, twice. Both Karolina Kurkovva and Heidi Klum wore highly embellished, highly sheer gowns; not that they haven’t worn less in public before—thanks, Victoria’s Secret fashion shows. Kurkova’s Cavalli number was inspired by Goldfinger, she Tweeted; as for Klum’s inspiration, the jury’s still out.
